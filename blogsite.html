Living a Fulfilling Life

#LifeTalks

I realized that living in this world is only temporary. It might be a long or short journey---we never know how long we will live here. It is scary to realize that, despite taking good care of our health and avoiding accidents, we can never be certain what might end our lives or how long we will live. We wake up every morning and go to sleep in the evening, following this routine in a continuous loop until one day, it suddenly stops. Suddenly, the people around you can neither see nor talk to you because your physical body is no longer moving and is dead..
